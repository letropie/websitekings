Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our web development services

What web development services do you provide?

WEBKINGS offers website development, e-commerce solutions, mobile app development, UI/UX design, custom web applications, and ongoing website maintenance and support.

How long does it take to build a website?

Project timelines depend on complexity. Standard websites take 2-3 DAYS, while larger or custom projects may take 1 week. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing varies by project scope. Basic websites start at $30, while advanced solutions can be $60 or more. We offer transparent quotes after discussing your needs.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes, we provide maintenance plans including updates, security, backups, and technical support. Plans are available monthly, quarterly, or annually.

Can you help improve my website's SEO?

Absolutely. We offer SEO services such as technical optimization, content strategy, and performance tracking to help your website rank higher in search engines.

How do I start a project with WEBKINGS?

Contact us for a free consultation. We'll discuss your goals, provide a proposal, and guide you through design, development, and launch with clear communication throughout.
